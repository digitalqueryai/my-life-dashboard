Daily Diet · Bengali + UK

食事 · balanced & calm

A simple, balanced day mixing Bengali home food with easy UK options — built around your routine. Two quiet rules: half your plate veg, and 腹八分目 hara hachi bu — stop at 80% full. More fish, dal & veg; fewer fried snacks & sweets. From your June check: this also lowers your triglycerides, cholesterol & liver enzymes — lean on oily fish & oats, and go easy on sugar, fried food & ghee. See the Health tab for the full plan.

🌅 Early start

after puja · ~7:00

Warm water with lemon, + 4 soaked almonds or 2 dates. No caffeine yet — wake the body gently.

🍳 Breakfast

8:30

🇧🇩 Ruti + egg bhurji / cholar dal.
🇬🇧 Oats porridge + banana & nuts, or wholegrain toast + eggs + grilled tomato. + tea.

🍎 Mid-morning

~11:00

Seasonal fruit + a few nuts, or a small bowl of doi (yogurt). Steady energy — skip the biscuits.

🍛 Lunch — main meal

12:30

🇧🇩 Bhat + dal + sabji + maach / chicken.
🇬🇧 Brown rice or jacket potato + grilled chicken/beans + a big salad.
Half plate veg · stop at 80%.

🍵 Evening tea

~17:00

Cha + a light snack: muri / chire, roasted chana, or one digestive. Keep it small; herbal tea if it's late.

🍲 Dinner — light & early

19:45

🇧🇩 2 ruti + sabji + dal, or grilled fish + veg.
🇬🇧 Vegetable soup + wholegrain toast, or grilled chicken + veg.
Lighter & earlier = better sleep.

💧

Through the day: 2–2.5 L water; oily fish 2× a week; limit fried food, sugar & late caffeine; cook at home when you can and eat seasonal (旬 shun). General healthy-eating guidance, not medical advice — adjust to your body and any doctor's advice.
